Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how GradeUp NIL collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Information Collection

GradeUp NIL collects information to provide and improve our services. We collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide

  • Account registration information (name, email, password)
  • Profile information (school, sport, position, academic records)
  • Payment and billing information
  • Communications with us or other users
  • Content you upload (photos, videos, documents)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, pages viewed)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Usage patterns and preferences

Information from Third Parties

  • Academic verification services
  • Social media platforms (when you connect accounts)
  • Athletic program data from schools
  • Identity verification providers

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To verify your student-athlete status and academic credentials
  • To connect athletes with brand partnership opportunities
  • To process payments and transactions
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To send notifications about opportunities, deals, and platform updates
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To ensure NCAA compliance and platform integrity
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We share your information in the following circumstances:

With Brands and Partners

When you apply for opportunities or express interest in partnerships, we share relevant profile information with brands. This may include your name, school, sport, GPA, and media assets.

With Service Providers

We work with trusted third parties who help us operate our platform, including payment processors, verification services, analytics providers, and hosting services.

With Athletic Programs

We may share information with your school's athletic compliance office as required for NCAA compliance.

For Legal Purposes

We may disclose information when required by law, to protect our rights, or to comply with legal processes.

With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection practices
  • Incident response procedures for potential breaches

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you of any breach affecting your data as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you and receive it in a commonly used format.

Correction

You can update or correct inaccurate information through your account settings or by contacting us.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time through your account settings or by clicking unsubscribe in emails.

Do Not Track

We respond to Do Not Track signals from your browser where technically feasible.

California Residents

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

6. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@gradeupnil.com
  • Address: GradeUp NIL, Inc., 123 Scholar Way, Durham, NC 27701

For data protection inquiries, you may also reach our Data Protection Officer at dpo@gradeupnil.com.

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting a notice on our platform or sending you an email. Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
