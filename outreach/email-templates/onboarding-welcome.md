- Custom [School Name] branded portal setup

---

### Week 2: Configuration

- **Platform Customization:** We'll configure GradeUp to match your GPA thresholds, semester schedules, and reward tiers
- **Compliance Review:** Our team will work with your compliance office to ensure all processes meet NCAA and state requirements
- **Admin Training:** A 45-minute training session for your admin team

---

### Week 3: Launch

- **Student-Athlete Onboarding:** We'll host a virtual (or in-person) session to introduce GradeUp to your athletes
- **Go-Live Support:** Our team will be on standby to answer questions and resolve any issues
- **Success Metrics Baseline:** We'll establish baseline metrics for measuring impact
