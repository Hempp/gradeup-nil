## Demo Script Outline

### Opening (2 min)
- Thank them for their time
- Confirm what they know about GradeUp
- Ask about their current NIL approach

### Problem (3 min)
- NIL challenges for academic-focused programs
- Gap in market for academic-first NIL
- Share stat: "94% of ADs concerned about NIL's academic impact"

### Solution (5 min)
- Show GradeUp platform
- Walk through athlete journey
- Explain GPA tier system
- Show brand matching

### Results (2 min)
- Share early metrics (0.3 GPA increase)
- Explain the business model
- Address compliance

### Close (3 min)
- Ask about their priorities
- Propose pilot program
- Define next steps
