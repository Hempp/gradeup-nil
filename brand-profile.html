About Apex Sports Co.

Apex Sports Co. is a leading athletic apparel and performance gear company dedicated to empowering the next generation of athletes. Founded in 2015, we believe that athletic excellence and academic achievement go hand in hand. Our mission is to provide cutting-edge performance gear while supporting scholar-athletes who represent the best of both worlds.

We partner exclusively with student-athletes who maintain strong academic standing, believing that discipline in the classroom translates to discipline on the field. As a Gold Scholar Sponsor, we are committed to investing in athletes who inspire through both their athletic prowess and their dedication to education.
