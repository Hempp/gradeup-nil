About Marcus

I'm a junior point guard at Duke University, balancing my passion for basketball with a commitment to academic excellence. Growing up in Atlanta, I learned that success on the court means nothing without growth off it. I maintain a 3.87 GPA while leading our team in assists and steals. My goal is to inspire the next generation of student-athletes to prioritize both their education and their sport.

I'm particularly passionate about financial literacy and youth mentorship. Through my NIL partnerships, I aim to work with brands that share my values of education, hard work, and community impact. I believe every deal should tell a story and make a difference.
