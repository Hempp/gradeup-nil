What is GradeUp?

The Smarter Way to NIL

GradeUp is the first NIL marketplace that factors academic performance into athlete valuations. We believe the best brand ambassadors are those who excel both on and off the field.

Our proprietary GradeUp Score combines athletic performance, social reach, and academic achievement to create a holistic view of each athlete's value - giving brands access to well-rounded student-athletes who represent the best of college sports.

Our Key Differentiator

GPA-weighted athlete valuations mean higher-performing students earn more. A 4.0 GPA can boost an athlete's earning potential by up to 40%.
